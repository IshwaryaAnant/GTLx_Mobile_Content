A block sits at rest on a frictionless surface. Which of the following sketches most closely resembles the correct freebody diagram for all forces acting on the block? Each red arror represents a force. Oberve their number and direction, but ignore their lengths.


A
B
C
D

Answer: A


Explanation: The box is at rest, so no net force is being applied vertically or horizontally. Vertically, however, there is the force of gravity pulling the block down and the normal force from the surface pushing the block up. These two forces are equal in magnitude and opposite in direction, thus the net force is indeed zero.
