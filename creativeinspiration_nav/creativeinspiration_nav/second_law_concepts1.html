Motion vs. Change in Motion

Newton's 2nd Law does not state what is required for something to be in motion. (The origin of an object's motion depends on the forces it experienced in the past.) The 2nd Law specifically quantifies the rate of change of motion (acceleration) any object will undergo as a consequence of the net force from all of the forces acting on it.
